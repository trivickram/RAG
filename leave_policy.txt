COMPANY LEAVE POLICY

1. INTRODUCTION
This leave policy outlines the guidelines and procedures for employees requesting time off from work. We recognize the importance of work-life balance and provide various types of leave to support our employees' well-being.

2. ANNUAL LEAVE (PAID TIME OFF - PTO)
- Eligibility: All full-time employees are eligible for PTO.
- Accrual: Employees accrue 1.5 days of PTO per month, totaling 18 days per year.
- Carryover: A maximum of 5 unused PTO days can be carried over to the next calendar year.
- Approval: PTO requests must be submitted at least two weeks in advance and approved by the direct supervisor.

3. SICK LEAVE
- Allocation: Employees are granted 10 days of paid sick leave per calendar year.
- Usage: Sick leave can be used for personal illness, medical appointments, or caring for an immediate family member who is ill.
- Documentation: A medical certificate may be required for sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days.

4. MATERNITY AND PATERNITY LEAVE
- Maternity Leave: Eligible female employees are entitled to 12 weeks of paid maternity leave.
- Paternity Leave: Eligible male employees are entitled to 4 weeks of paid paternity leave.
- Advance Notice: Employees must provide at least 30 days' notice before the expected start date.

5. BEREAVEMENT LEAVE
- Employees are entitled to up to 5 days of paid bereavement leave in the event of the death of an immediate family member (spouse, child, parent, sibling).

6. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
- The company observes all standard national public holidays. A calendar of observed holidays will be distributed at the beginning of each year.
