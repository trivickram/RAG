CUSTOMER REFUND POLICY

1. OVERVIEW
We want our customers to be completely satisfied with their purchases. If you are not entirely happy with your purchase, we're here to help.

2. RETURNS
- Timeframe: You have 30 calendar days to return an item from the date you received it.
- Condition: To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused, in the same condition that you received it, and in its original packaging.
- Receipt: Your item needs to have the receipt or proof of purchase.

3. REFUNDS
- Process: Once we receive your item, we will inspect it and notify you that we have received your returned item. We will immediately notify you on the status of your refund after inspecting the item.
- Approval: If your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your credit card (or original method of payment).
- Timing: You will receive the credit within a certain amount of days, depending on your card issuer's policies.

4. EXCHANGES
- We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange it for the same item, please contact our customer support team.

5. NON-REFUNDABLE ITEMS
- Certain types of items cannot be returned, including:
  * Perishable goods
  * Downloadable software products
  * Gift cards
  * Items on clearance sale

6. SHIPPING COSTS
- You will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund.

7. CONTACT US
If you have any questions on how to return your item to us, please contact our customer service team at support@examplecompany.com or call 1-800-555-0199.
